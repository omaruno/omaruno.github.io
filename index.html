Research Interests

AI for Exoskeletons

Developing a system that enables an exoskeleton to recognize the terrain it's on, improving safety and adapting its movement.

Self-Pretraining Models

Investigating self-supervised and pretraining methodologies to create robust, adaptable models for healthcare that require minimal labeled data.

Application of AI to Biology

Applying advanced deep learning techniques for the analysis of genomic and single-cell sequencing data to advance precision medicine.

Mental Health via AI

Utilizing machine learning to analyze behavioral and physiological data for early detection and personalized prevention of mental health issues.
